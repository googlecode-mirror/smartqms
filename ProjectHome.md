The SmartQMS application provides much more than a superb document
management solution. It also includes capabilities for ensuring regulatory
compliance through quality management and internal auditing with the ability to link
policies and procedures to applicable regulatory standards which can be fully
integrated into SmartQMS. Equipment maintenance, reagent ordering, using, staff education, training, competency evaluation, meetings management are also key features  within SmartQMS which delivers a task based approach to total quality management for laboratory.
